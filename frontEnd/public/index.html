Our Purpose

The purpose of Fitness Radar is to allow users to track their fitness activity at different locations. Since its the summer and the weather is warm, there is no better time than now to go outside to stay healthy! All you have to do is log in or sign up for an account to get started. Using Fitness Radar, you can not only track specific information about your fitness activities, but also challenge others near you!
